About me

Eqan Ahmad, is currently pursuing BS Computer Science from one of the most prestigious university of Pakistan, FAST NUCES. He aspires to be a Full stack Developer also arming with AI & Automation Capabilities to make every day tasks at ease.

He seeks towards eliminating repitition in daily tasks with the aid of Software & AI. He is passionate about Programming in General, the Code Structure & the complexity of the code has been enticing towards his career.

He also puts his creative side in formulating minimal UI/UX projects as he has a certain passion for developing minimal, user friendly and fully functional software.
